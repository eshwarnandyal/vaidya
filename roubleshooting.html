Vaidya AI – Troubleshooting Guide

Quick solutions for common issues

Problem Solution
Dashboard graphs are not loading. Check your internet connection. Pull down on the screen to refresh the dashboard.
V-Code command not working. Speak clearly (e.g., “Vee One,” not “V one”). V-codes work best from the main dashboard screen.
Patient or RMP name not recognized. Speak the name exactly as registered. Reduce background noise. You can also manually tap the name on the screen.
Call does not go through. Ensure Vaidya AI has permission to “Make and manage phone calls” in phone settings.
Voice-to-text messaging is inaccurate. Speak moderately in a quiet place. Poor internet can also affect transcription quality.
